Frequently Asked Questions

How do I customize my medicine schedule?
Tap the settings icon (gear) in the top right corner of the main screen. You can enable or disable any timing that suits your schedule.
How do I change the app theme?
Tap the palette icon in the top right corner to access theme options. Free themes include Luxury and Minimal. Premium themes (Japanese Modern, Pop, Dark Mode) are available with a subscription.
How does the free version work?
The free version includes 3 complimentary taps upon installation. After that, you can watch a short ad to unlock 24 hours of unlimited use, or subscribe to Premium for an ad-free experience.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can manage your subscription through your device settings:

iOS: Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions
Android: Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions
Is my data private?
Yes! All your medicine tracking data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or have access to your personal health information. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.
The app is not working properly on my iPad
For the best experience on iPad, we recommend using the Premium version. Some features may be limited in the free version on iPad devices.

Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes

Try these steps:

  1. Close the app completely and restart it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Make sure you have the latest version installed
  4. If the problem persists, please contact us with details about your device and iOS/Android version

Subscription not recognized

If you've subscribed but the app doesn't recognize it:

  1. Make sure you're signed in with the correct Apple ID or Google account
  2. Try the "Restore Purchases" button in the app settings
  3. Wait a few minutes as sometimes it takes time to process
  4. Contact us if the issue persists
